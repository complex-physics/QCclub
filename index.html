1.1. 预读班的主题

  1. 量子线路与量子过程的经典模拟 (efficient classical simulation of quantum circuit & quantum dynamics)
  2. 量子纠错码 (quantum error-correction code)
  3. 量子与智能 (Artificial intelligence meets quantum computing)
  4. 量子时间晶体 (quantum time crystal)
  5. 量子实验--光量子信息处理方向(quantum experiment--the direction of optical quantum information processing)
  6. 量子基础 (quantum foundation)
  7. 量子模拟 (quantum simulation)
  8. 量子噪声理论 (quantum noise model and noise mitigation)
  9. 量子算法及量子优势 (quantum algorithm and quantum advantage)
  10. 量子程序的编译和优化（quantum compilation and optimization)
